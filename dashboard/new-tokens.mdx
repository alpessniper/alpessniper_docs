## Overview

The **Token NEW** section displays signals for newly launched tokens between 0-48 hours old.

<Info>
  New tokens have the highest potential risk and reward. They're discovered early but have less historical data.
</Info>

## What You'll See

Each signal card shows:
- Token symbol and name
- Age (always < 48 hours)
- Market cap at entry
- Current market cap
- 24h trading volume
- Number of holders
- Liquidity amount
- DEX Paid status
- Social links

## Use Cases

**Best for:**
- Traders seeking early entry opportunities
- Those comfortable with higher risk/reward
- Quick decision makers

**Consider:**
- Limited historical data available
- Higher volatility
- Faster price movements

## Tips for New Tokens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Socials">
    Verify the team has active social channels
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Holders">
    Look for healthy holder distribution
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assess Liquidity">
    Ensure there's enough liquidity for your trade size
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Limits">
    Define your profit target and stop-loss before entering
  </Step>
</Steps>
