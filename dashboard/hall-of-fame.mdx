## Overview

The **Hall of Fame** showcases our best performing signals - the ones that achieved exceptional returns.

## What Qualifies

Signals appear in Hall of Fame based on:
- Exceptional ROI performance
- Significant market cap growth
- Notable multipliers achieved

## Displayed Information

Each Hall of Fame entry shows:
- Token symbol and name
- Entry market cap
- ATH (All-Time High) market cap
- Maximum ROI achieved
- Date of signal
- Time to reach ATH

## Using Hall of Fame

<Steps>
  <Step title="Learn from Winners">
    Study what made these signals successful
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify Patterns">
    Look for common characteristics in top performers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Benchmarks">
    Understand what exceptional performance looks like
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Notes

<Warning>
  Hall of Fame shows the best outcomes, which are not typical. Most signals don't achieve these levels. Use this for inspiration, not as an expectation.
</Warning>

**Remember:**
- These are exceptional cases, not the norm
- Many factors contribute to outlier performance
- Past winners don't predict future winners
- Always maintain realistic expectations
