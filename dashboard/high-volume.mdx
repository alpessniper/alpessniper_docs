## Overview

These sections organize signals by **token age**, helping you find tokens at different stages of their lifecycle.

## Tokens >3D

Tokens between **3-7 days** old.

**Characteristics:**
- Survived initial launch phase
- Early community forming
- Trading patterns emerging
- More data available than new tokens

**Best for:** Balanced risk/reward traders

---

## Tokens >7D

Tokens between **7-30 days** old.

**Characteristics:**
- More stable price action
- Clearer community trajectory
- Better liquidity typically
- Week+ of historical data

**Best for:** Moderate risk traders

---

## Tokens >30D

Tokens **30+ days** old.

**Characteristics:**
- Survived multiple market cycles
- Stronger community typically
- More predictable patterns
- Established track record

**Best for:** Conservative traders seeking stability

---

## Comparing Age Categories

| Category | Risk Level | Data Available | Typical Volatility |
|----------|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Token NEW | Very High | Minimal | Very High |
| Tokens >3D | High | Some | High |
| Tokens >7D | Medium | Good | Medium |
| Tokens >30D | Lower | Extensive | Lower |
