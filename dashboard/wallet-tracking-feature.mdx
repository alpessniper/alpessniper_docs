## Overview

**Wallet Tracking** is a personal monitoring feature that allows you to track up to **5 wallets** of your choice. The system monitors these wallets every **60 seconds** and alerts you when swaps (buys or sells) are detected.

<Info>
  Available with **Starter** tier and above (5 wallets).
</Info>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Wallets">
    Enter the Solana wallet addresses you want to monitor
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-Time Monitoring">
    System checks each wallet every 60 seconds
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swap Detection">
    When a buy or sell swap is detected, you receive an alert
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Activity">
    See the token, amount, and transaction details
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Up Wallet Tracking

### Add a Wallet

1. Go to **Profile** → **Wallet Tracking**
2. Click **Add Wallet**
3. Paste the Solana wallet address
4. Give it a nickname (optional)
5. Click **Save**

### Remove a Wallet

1. Go to **Profile** → **Wallet Tracking**
2. Find the wallet you want to remove
3. Click the **Remove** button
4. Confirm removal

| Plan | Wallets Allowed |

## What Gets Tracked

When a monitored wallet makes a swap, you'll see:

- **Token** - Which token was bought or sold
- **Action** - Buy or Sell
- **Amount** - Transaction size
- **Timestamp** - When it happened
- **Transaction** - Link to explorer

## Important Notes

<Warning>
  Wallet Tracking monitors wallets you specify. This is different from **Signals by Wallet**, which shows signals from Smart Money wallets we track based on performance.
</Warning>

- Monitoring interval: 60 seconds
- Maximum wallets: 5 per account
- Detects: Token swaps (buys and sells)
- Does not detect: NFT transactions, transfers, staking

## Privacy

- Your tracked wallets are private to your account
- We don't share tracking lists with other users
- Wallet owners are not notified of being tracked
