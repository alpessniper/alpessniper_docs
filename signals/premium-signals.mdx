## What Are Premium Signals?

Premium Signals are **curated, high-conviction opportunities** that meet stricter criteria than regular signals. These are designed for traders who want quality over quantity.

## How to Access

1. Subscribe to the **Premium** tier
2. Navigate to **Premium Signals** in the sidebar
3. Premium badges also appear on qualifying signals in other sections

## Premium Signal Badge

Look for the **crown badge** on signal cards:

- Appears next to the token symbol
- Gold/amber colored
- Indicates the signal met Premium criteria at generation time
