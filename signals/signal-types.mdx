## Overview

Alpes Sniper provides signals through three main discovery methods, plus Premium signals for high-conviction opportunities.

---

## Signals by Engine

Engine signals are based on **token age** - different time windows capture tokens at various stages of their lifecycle.

### Token NEW

<Info>
  Tokens between **0-48 hours** old
</Info>

These are the freshest token launches. Characteristics:
- Highest potential risk and reward
- Early discovery opportunity
- Requires quick decision making
- Best for experienced traders

**Best for:** Traders looking to catch tokens at launch

---

### Tokens >3D

<Info>
  Tokens **3-7 days** old
</Info>

Tokens that have survived the initial launch phase:
- Initial volatility has settled
- Early community forming
- Trading patterns emerging
- More data available for analysis

**Best for:** Balanced risk/reward seekers

---

### Tokens >7D

<Info>
  Tokens **7-30 days** old
</Info>

Tokens establishing market presence:
- More stable price action
- Clearer community trajectory
- Better liquidity typically
- Historical data to analyze

**Best for:** Moderate risk traders

---

### Tokens >30D

<Info>
  Tokens **30+ days** old
</Info>

Established tokens with proven track record:
- Survived multiple market cycles
- Stronger community typically
- More predictable patterns
- Lower risk, potentially lower reward

**Best for:** Conservative traders seeking stability

---

## Signals by Wallet

### Smart Money Signals

Signals generated when **our tracked** high-performance wallets interact with tokens.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="What It Tracks" icon="wallet">
    Wallets with proven trading performance that we monitor
  </Card>
  <Card title="Why It Matters" icon="brain">
    Experienced traders often identify opportunities early
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Key features:**
- Real-time wallet monitoring
- Historical performance data on tracked wallets
- Multiple wallet confirmation possible

<Note>
  These are wallets **we** track based on performance. For tracking your **own** wallets, see [Wallet Tracking](/dashboard/wallet-tracking-feature).
</Note>

## Signals by Volume

### Tokens High Mcap

High market cap tokens with significant trading volume.

<Info>
  Established tokens with substantial market presence
</Info>

**Characteristics:**
- Higher market capitalization
- Significant trading volume
- Better liquidity
- Lower volatility typically

**Best for:** Traders seeking lower-risk opportunities in established tokens

---

## Premium Signals

<Warning>
  Premium tier subscription required
</Warning>

Premium signals are **high-conviction opportunities** that meet additional criteria:

- Stronger validation metrics
- Multiple positive indicators aligned
- Higher confidence scoring

Premium signals appear across all categories with a special badge.

---

## Feature: Wallet Tracking

<Info>
  This is a **personal monitoring feature**, not a signal type.
</Info>

Wallet Tracking lets you monitor up to **5 wallets** of your choice:
- You choose which wallets to track
- System monitors every **60 seconds**
- Alerts you on buy/sell swaps

**See:** [Wallet Tracking Feature](/dashboard/wallet-tracking-feature)

## Choosing the Right Signal Type

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assess Your Risk Tolerance">
    New tokens = higher risk, established = lower risk
  </Step>
  <Step title="Consider Your Time">
    New tokens need quick decisions, older tokens allow more research time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match Your Strategy">
    Day traders may prefer NEW, swing traders may prefer >7D
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diversify">
    Don't put all eggs in one basket - mix signal types
  </Step>
</Steps>
