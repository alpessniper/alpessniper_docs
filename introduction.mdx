## What is Alpes Sniper?

Alpes Sniper is a **real-time Solana token discovery platform** designed for traders who want to identify promising tokens early. Our system continuously monitors the Solana blockchain and delivers curated signals based on multiple analysis criteria.

## How It Works

1. **Discovery** - Our system continuously scans the Solana blockchain for new and trending tokens
2. **Analysis** - Each token goes through multiple validation layers
3. **Signal** - Qualifying tokens are delivered as signals to your dashboard
4. **Tracking** - We continue monitoring performance and update metrics
