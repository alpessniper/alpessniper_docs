## What is Alpes Sniper?

Alpes Sniper is a **real-time Solana token discovery platform** powered by artificial intelligence. Designed for traders who want to identify promising tokens early, our AI-enhanced system continuously monitors the Solana blockchain and delivers curated signals with reduced noise and higher quality.

## How It Works

1. **Discovery** - Our system continuously scans the Solana blockchain for new and trending tokens
2. **AI Analysis** - Each token goes through AI-powered validation to filter noise and identify quality
3. **Signal** - Qualifying tokens are delivered as signals to your dashboard in real-time
4. **Tracking** - We continue monitoring performance and update metrics automatically
