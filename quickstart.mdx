## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the App">
    Go to [app.alpessniper.com](https://app.alpessniper.com) and click **Get Started**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create an account using:
    - **Email** - Traditional email/password signup
    - **Wallet** - Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
    - **Social** - Google, Discord, or Twitter login
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    If using email signup, verify your email address
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choose Your Plan

After signing up, you'll start with limited access. To unlock full features:

1. Navigate to **Pricing** page
2. Select a plan that fits your needs
3. Complete payment via Stripe (card) or crypto

<Info>
  All plans include a **7-day money-back guarantee**. Try risk-free!
</Info>

## Navigate the Dashboard

Once subscribed, you'll have access to:

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **New Tokens** | Recently launched tokens (0-48h) |
| **High Volume 3D** | Tokens with significant 3-day volume |
| **High Volume 7D** | Tokens with significant 7-day volume |
| **Wallet Signals** | Tokens from tracked Smart Money wallets |
| **Premium Signals** | High-conviction signals (Premium tier) |
| **Performance** | Historical performance metrics |

## Understanding Signal Cards

Each signal card displays:

- **Token Symbol & Name** - Token identification
- **Market Cap** - Current market capitalization
- **Entry MCAP** - Market cap when signal was generated
- **Volume 24h** - Trading volume in last 24 hours
- **Holders** - Number of token holders
- **Age** - Time since token creation
- **DEX Paid** - Whether token has enhanced info on DEX

<Tip>
  Click on any card to expand and see more details including social links and contract address.
</Tip>

## Quick Actions

### Copy Contract Address
Click the contract address to copy it to your clipboard for trading on DEX platforms.

### View on Explorer
Click the explorer icon to view the token on Solscan or Solana Explorer.

### Trade Links
Direct links to trade on popular DEX aggregators are provided on each card.
