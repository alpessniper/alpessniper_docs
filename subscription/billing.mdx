## Payment Processing

All payments are securely processed through **Stripe**, a PCI-compliant payment processor.

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- (Crypto payments coming soon)

---

## Billing Cycle

### Monthly Billing
- Charged on the same day each month
- Example: Subscribe Jan 15 → Charged Feb 15, Mar 15, etc.

### Annual Billing
- Charged once per year
- 25% discount applied
- Example: Subscribe Jan 15 → Next charge Jan 15 next year

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Subscription Status

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Click on **Subscription** section
3. View current plan, next billing date, payment method

### Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. Update card details in Stripe portal

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Access continues until current billing period ends

<Note>
  When you cancel, you keep access until your current billing period ends. No partial refunds for cancellations.
</Note>

## Upgrades & Downgrades

### Upgrading

When you upgrade:
1. Immediate access to new features
2. Prorated charge for remaining days
3. New billing cycle at higher rate

**Example:**
- Current: Starter ($59/mo), 15 days left
- Upgrade to: Plus ($99/mo)
- Prorated charge: ~$20 (difference for 15 days)
- Next full charge: $99 in 15 days

### Downgrading

When you downgrade:
1. Keep current access until billing cycle ends
2. New plan activates at next billing date
3. No prorated refunds

## Invoices & Receipts

### Access Invoices

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Billing History**
3. Download PDF invoices

### Invoice Contents

Each invoice includes:
- Transaction date
- Plan details
- Amount charged
- Payment method (last 4 digits)
- Unique invoice number

## Refund Policy

<Warning>
  All sales are final. No refunds are provided for subscriptions.
</Warning>

**Why no refunds:**
- Digital services are delivered immediately upon purchase
- API plans (Agent AI) involve real resource consumption
- You can cancel anytime to prevent future charges

**Before subscribing:**
- Review all plan features on our website
- Choose the plan that fits your needs
- Contact support if you have questions

## Common Issues

  <Accordion title="Payment failed">
    - Check card details are correct
    - Ensure sufficient funds
    - Try a different card
    - Contact your bank if blocks persist
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Charged twice">
    Contact support immediately with:
    - Your email
    - Transaction dates
    - Amounts charged
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't access after payment">
    - Wait 2-3 minutes for processing
    - Refresh the page
    - Log out and log back in
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
